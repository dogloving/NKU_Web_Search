A Brief introduction of the Nankai University Alumni Association

Nankai University Alumni Association has a long history. In December 1919, Zhou Enlai presided over the establishment of a department for a long-standing contact with those graduated students and he worked as the first manager for the alumni contacting. In 1925, President Zhang Boling proposed to organise an alumni association to contact the graduating students. After ten years’ accumulation, the formal Nankai University Alumni established on October 1929. The Alumni Association soon became the largest, the best management, and the best effect all over the alumni organizations, which be called as "the first Alumni Association” in China. However, due to the historical reasons, Nankai University Aliumni Association’s activities once be interrupted. By the strongly appeal from majority of the alumni, it continue to work in 1979 and registered in the Ministry of Civil Affairs in 1992 to be as an independent corporation to continue to carry out activities.

Nankai University Alumni Association has 11 members, which are Nankai University, Tianjin Nankai High School, Tianjin Second Nankai High School, Chongqing Nankai High School, Zigong Shuguang Middle School, Nankai University Binhai College, Tianjin Nankai Xiangyu Middle School, Tianjin Xingnan High School, Chongqing Nankai (Rongqiao) Middle School, The Attached High School of Nankai University, and Affiliated Primary School of Nankai University. It has formed a four dimensions of Organization, named the Local Alumni Association, the Acadamic Alumni Association, Profession Alumni Association, and grade Council. The local Alumni Association (including those which are in plan) across more than 50 provinces and cities in China, and ...

A Brief introduction of the Nankai University Alumni Association

Nankai University Alumni Association has a long history. In December 1919, Zhou Enlai presided over the establishment of a department for a long-standing contact with those graduated students and he worked as the first manager for the alumni contacting. In 1925, President Zhang Boling proposed to organise an alumni association to contact the graduating students. After ten years’ accumulation, the formal Nankai University Alumni established on October 1929. The Alumni Association soon became the largest, the best management, and the best effect all over the alumni organizations, which be called as "the first Alumni Association” in China. However, due to the historical reasons, Nankai University Aliumni Association’s activities once be interrupted. By the strongly appeal from majority of the alumni, it continue to work in 1979 and registered in the Ministry of Civil Affairs in 1992 to be as an independent corporation to continue to carry out activities.

Nankai University Alumni Association has 11 members, which are Nankai University, Tianjin Nankai High School, Tianjin Second Nankai High School, Chongqing Nankai High School, Zigong Shuguang Middle School, Nankai University Binhai College, Tianjin Nankai Xiangyu Middle School, Tianjin Xingnan High School, Chongqing Nankai (Rongqiao) Middle School, The Attached High School of Nankai University, and Affiliated Primary School of Nankai University. It has formed a four dimensions of Organization, named the Local Alumni Association, the Acadamic Alumni Association, Profession Alumni Association, and grade Council. The local Alumni Association (including those which are in plan) across more than 50 provinces and cities in China, and also established more than 20 overseas Alumni association which located in the United States, Britain, Canada, Russia, Japan, Singapore, South Korea, and other countries and regions.
